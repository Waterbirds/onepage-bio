Internship Project

The goal of the internship project was to research and produce reports and implementation of various Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML) models. Providing Castlepoint with the necessary information to make an informed decision, when choosing which NLP & ML methods to implement into their records management system.

Key Phrase Extraction

  • Researched a multitude of natural language processing and key phrase extraction models. Selecting the best models based on speed, resource requirements and accuracy. Testing to ensure robustness against a variety of datasets including documents of different lengths and types.
  • Once selected implemented in Python and then containerised into a Docker image. Allowing for easy scalability on cloud services such as AWS & Microsoft Azure. As well providing ease of maintainability.
